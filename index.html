Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to The MotoMate privacy policy. This policy outlines how The MotoMate (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services.

We do not collect any personal data from users through our app. All data is collected from the physical devices installed in users' vehicles. This may include but is not limited to vehicle performance metrics, maintenance data, and location information.

3. Use of Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of providing and improving our services, including but not limited to enhancing user experience, analyzing trends, and ensuring the proper functioning of our products.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting the security of your information and take reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of data. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Compliance with Laws

We comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding the collection, use, and retention of data. This includes but is not limited to privacy laws and regulations such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@themotomate.com.
